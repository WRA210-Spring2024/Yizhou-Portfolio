About Yizhou

Hi, thanks for delving into this section of my site!

I am a current undergraduate student based at East Lansing, Michigan. I study Zoology, concentration in ecology & evolution. I have been developing myself as a photographer, primarily documenting the amazing interaction between habitats and species on my journeys around the world.

I am passionate about broadening the way I view the world, bringing my senses into the habitat, and of course, I am passionate about sharing those creatures that grace this wondrous land. If you would like to find out more, please feel free to contact me for anything via liuyizh3@msu.edu
